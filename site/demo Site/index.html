About

In this current age and time where data protection is as important as the food we eat due to the presence of software vulnerabilities and the like, user information protection has become a very important topic to many companies. Heath sectors are no exception. Most of these health institutions have centralized systems that store all their user information and health records. Great right? No! A centralized system although great in the 19th century now increases the risk of losing confidential patient information to hackers and other malicious intentioned persons. Therefore, Prediit is a decentralized Healthcare system that minimizes if not prevent unwanted access to patient information to the highest degree. Assuring patients that their personal health information is in safe hands. An added feature to Prediit is that predictions of various kinds of diseases can be made with the click of a button.
